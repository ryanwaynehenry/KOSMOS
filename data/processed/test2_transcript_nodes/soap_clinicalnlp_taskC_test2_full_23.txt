## RESULTS

## ASSESSMENT AND PLAN
Right elbow pain consistent with medial epicondylitis (overuse/tendon-related). [74][78]  
Plan for elbow: rest and activity modification including holding off on pottery for the next couple of weeks, order a right arm sling to wear while awake, apply ice for 20 minutes three times daily, and take ibuprofen 600 mg (3 pills) every 6 hours with food for 1 week. [74]  
If symptoms are taking longer to heal, consider physical rehabilitation/therapy and discuss at follow-up. [82]  
Diabetes mellitus with suboptimal adherence and elevated home glucose readings: increase metformin to 1000 mg twice daily, check and record blood sugars daily, order hemoglobin A1c, and follow up in a couple of weeks to reassess control and consider additional options if needed. [76]  
Refer to diabetic counseling/dietitian for updated nutrition guidance and dietary management support. [46][47][76]  
Hypertension: continue lisinopril 10 mg daily given improved in-office BP and reported home readings. [24][25][50][76]
