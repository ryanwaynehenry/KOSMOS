## ASSESSMENT AND PLAN
Bilateral knee pain most consistent with patellofemoral pain syndrome based on history, exam, and imaging review. [34]  
Recommend physical therapy for lower-extremity strengthening/mobility and to address running mechanics. [34]  
May continue ibuprofen/other anti-inflammatory medication as needed for pain. [34]  
Advise taking it easy from running for now, with goal to return to running as symptoms improve. [36][40]  
Plan follow-up soon and reassess progress at subsequent visits. [36][40]
