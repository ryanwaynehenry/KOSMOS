[40] Ice the elbow 3 times daily for 20 minutes each session. [40] Avoid playing lacrosse for the next couple of weeks to allow healing, then return to activity as tolerated. [40]

**2) Hypertension, elevated in clinic today.** [0][22] Continue lisinopril 20 mg daily and continue low-salt diet; clinician discussed possible white coat effect. [22][24][25][40]
