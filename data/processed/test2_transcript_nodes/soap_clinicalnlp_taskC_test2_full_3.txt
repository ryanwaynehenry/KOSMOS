- Order lipid panel and routine labs including a complete metabolic panel. [27][29]  

**2) Headaches:** Headaches present in the setting of elevated blood pressure and work stress; no chest pain or shortness of breath reported. [8][9][10][11]  
- Address by improving blood pressure control as above. [25]  

**3) Status post kidney transplant:** Patient reports transplant is stable and she is taking immunosuppression medications; kidney function is reassuring. [12][15][16][17][21][29]  
- Continue current transplant/immunosuppression medications. [29]  

**Follow-up:** Nurse to help schedule follow-up in a couple of weeks to reassess blood pressure response to the new medication. [30][31]
