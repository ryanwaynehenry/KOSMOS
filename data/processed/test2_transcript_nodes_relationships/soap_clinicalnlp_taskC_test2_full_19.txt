## RESULTS
Right shoulder X-ray (performed prior to clinician evaluation) showed an acute acromioclavicular joint fracture (small fracture of the acromion). [42]  

## ASSESSMENT AND PLAN

- Immobilize in a sling for now. [42]  
- Start meloxicam 15 mg by mouth once daily for pain; patient prefers to try this rather than a stronger medication. [42][44][45]  
- Order CT scan of the right shoulder to better evaluate the joint. [42]  
- Refer to physical therapy to strengthen the shoulder with goal of avoiding surgery; clinician does not think surgery is needed at this time. [42]
