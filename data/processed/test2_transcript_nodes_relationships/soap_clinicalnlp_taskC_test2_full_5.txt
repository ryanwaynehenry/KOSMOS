Start anti-inflammatory therapy with ibuprofen 600 mg four times daily with food. [44]  
Begin straight leg raise exercises to strengthen the quadriceps to help reduce pain. [44]  
Avoid activities that put extra pressure on the knees for now (no lunges and avoid breaststroke); if comfortable, switch to strokes with knees straight or take a break from swimming. [46]
