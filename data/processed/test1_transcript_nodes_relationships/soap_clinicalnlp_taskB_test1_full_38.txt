Plan: Order right knee MRI to evaluate for ACL tear and assess for other structural damage given lateral pain and exam findings. [32]

Support: Provide a knee brace for added stability/support. [38]  
Follow-up: Aim to obtain MRI within the next couple of days and then review results and discuss next steps. [38]
