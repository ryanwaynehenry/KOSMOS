Diet and exercise are “decent” but could be better; he is trying to watch carbs but is not counting and is not on keto. [45][47][49][51]

## RESULTS
Right knee x-ray showed no evidence of fracture or bony abnormality. [60]  

## ASSESSMENT AND PLAN
**1) Right knee pain – suspected lateral ligament strain.** [60]  
Start meloxicam 15 mg daily for pain and swelling. [60]  
Refer to physical therapy for strengthening and injury prevention. [60]  
If pain persists, consider further imaging. [60]  

**2) Hypertension – poorly controlled in clinic.** [33][34]  
Continue lisinopril 20 mg for now and order an echocardiogram. [60]  
Given elevated office BP, clinician noted possible need for medication modification. [34][60]  

**3) Diabetes mellitus.** [34][60]  
Order hemoglobin A1c to assess long-term glycemic control. [60]  
Order lipid panel to guide potential medication adjustments if needed. [60]  

Patient agreed with the plan and had no further questions. [61][62]
