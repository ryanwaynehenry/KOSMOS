Obtain MRI of the right knee to evaluate for PCL injury and assess for other internal derangements (e.g., meniscus or cartilage injury). [92][95]  
Initial management: knee brace fitting today (non-custom/temporary) and referral to physical therapy to restore motion and strength, with plan to reassess in 4–6 weeks. [92][110][112][113]  
Pain/swelling management: ice packs a few times per day and ibuprofen up to three times daily for a short period if helpful; clinician will review dosing. [98][100]  
Medication option: trial a prescription anti-inflammatory taken twice daily (with breakfast and dinner), with option to return to ibuprofen if it works better; prescription to be called into pharmacy. [102][104][106][108]
