## ASSESSMENT AND PLAN

Monitoring plan: Annual renal ultrasound to monitor kidney size/overall function, annual urinalysis to ensure no protein or blood in urine, and annual blood pressure check. [74]  
Risk counseling: Discussed higher risk for future hypertension and proteinuria and the need to keep an eye on kidney function and blood pressure. [70]

Follow-up: Next appointment can be with primary care or an adult nephrologist closer to home; advised to call if symptoms worsen or questions arise. [82][84]
