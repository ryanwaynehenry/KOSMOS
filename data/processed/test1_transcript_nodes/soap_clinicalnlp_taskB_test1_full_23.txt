## RESULTS
Right knee x-ray showed no evidence of fracture or bony abnormality. [60]  

## ASSESSMENT AND PLAN
**1) Right knee pain—suspected lateral ligament strain.** [60]  
Start meloxicam 15 mg daily for pain and swelling. [60]  
Refer to physical therapy to strengthen supporting muscles and help prevent further injury. [60]  
If pain persists, consider further imaging; clinician noted this is a common injury that often heals on its own. [60]  

**2) Hypertension—poorly controlled in clinic.** [33][34][60]  
Continue lisinopril 20 mg daily; clinician expressed concern BP is not at goal and may require medication modification. [30][60][34]  
Order an echocardiogram. [60]  

**3) Diabetes mellitus.** [34][60]  
Order hemoglobin A1c to assess long-term glycemic control. [60]  
Order lipid panel to guide possible medication adjustments if needed. [60]  

Patient agreed with the plan and had no questions. [61][62]
