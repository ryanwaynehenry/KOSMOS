Pain is isolated to the left shoulder. [10][11]

## RESULTS

Order MRI of the shoulder to evaluate for other pathology. [38]  
Refer to physical therapy for approximately 6–8 weeks and pursue conservative management. [38][40]  
Continue Tylenol as needed for pain during treatment. [38]  
If symptoms do not improve, consider a steroid injection to the left shoulder. [38]  
Follow up in about 2 weeks. [42]
